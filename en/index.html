FAQ

What customers ask before service or installation

How quickly can you come for service?

It depends on location, fault type and current workload. The fastest way is to call, briefly describe the issue and we will tell you the nearest possible date.

Do you service equipment you did not install?

Yes. We service and upgrade existing doors, gates, operators, automatic doors and barriers even if we did not originally install them.

Is repair or replacement better?

After inspection we recommend a sensible solution. If repair makes sense, we repair. If it would only be short-term or uneconomical, we recommend an upgrade or a new custom-made solution.

Can you deliver custom-made doors including the motor?

Yes. We handle consultation, measurement, design, manufacture, delivery, installation with motor, controls, commissioning and follow-up service.

What should I prepare before calling?

Location, equipment type, a short description of the issue and ideally a photo of the door, gate, operator or barrier.
