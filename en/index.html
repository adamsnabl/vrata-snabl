How to recognize a good solution

Diagnostics first, then a sensible solution

Not every problem means replacing the whole device. First we find the cause, then we recommend service, adjustment, operator replacement, modification of the existing gate or a new custom-made solution.

When the equipment will not open

We check the operator, controls, mechanics, safety elements and power supply. The goal is to see whether service, adjustment or part replacement is enough.

When the old solution keeps failing

We say clearly whether repair still makes sense. If not, we recommend an operator upgrade, controls upgrade or a complete new custom-made solution.

When you want custom doors or a gate

We measure the space, discuss operation and design the door, gate, motor, controls and accessories so the whole system works comfortably and safely.

When it is a company, apartment building or site

For operational buildings, we focus on reliability, entrance availability, safe movement and minimal disruption of daily operation.

FAQ

What customers ask before service or installation

How much do custom-made doors cost?

The price mainly depends on dimensions, construction, operator, controls, accessories and installation complexity. That is why it makes sense to discuss the space and requirements first, or arrange measurement.

What affects the price of service or repair most?

Equipment type, parts availability, condition of mechanics, operator, controls and safety elements matter, as well as whether it is a single fault or a recurring problem.

How often should garage doors or gates be serviced?

For frequently used doors and gates, regular inspection of movement, mechanics, operator and safety elements makes sense. For companies, apartment buildings and sites, prevention is more important than waiting for a fault.

How do I know the operator needs replacement?

Typical signs are irregular movement, stopping, unusual noise, remote-control problems, incorrect end positions or recurring faults even after adjustment.

How quickly can service be arranged?

It depends on location, fault type and current workload. The fastest way is to call, briefly describe the problem and agree the next step.

Do you repair older doors or gates?

Yes. We service, adjust and upgrade existing doors, gates, operators, automatic doors and barriers even if we did not originally install them.

Is repair or replacement better?

After inspection we recommend a sensible next step. If repair makes sense, we repair. If it would be short-term or uneconomical, we recommend an upgrade or a new custom-made solution.

Can you deliver doors including the operator?

Yes. We provide consultation, measurement, design, custom production, delivery, installation with motor, controls, commissioning and follow-up service.

Do you install operators for sliding and swing gates?

Yes. For suitable gates, we handle installation, service and adjustment of operators, motors, controls, safety elements and end positions.

Do you service barriers and automatic doors?

Yes. We service barriers, automatic doors and access systems mainly for companies, apartment buildings, institutions and sites.

What should I prepare before calling?

Location, equipment type, a short fault description and, ideally, a photo of the door, gate, operator or barrier. This helps us understand the situation faster.

Can I send a photo before service?

Yes. A photo of the door, gate, operator, barrier or automatic door often helps us understand whether the next step is service, adjustment, part replacement or a new solution.

Do you work outside Prague?

Yes. We mainly work in Prague and Central Bohemia, including the areas around Prague, Kladno, Beroun, Melnik, Benesov, Pribram, Kolin, Kutna Hora and Mlada Boleslav.

Can you help choose new custom-made doors?

Yes. We discuss space, usage, operator and control requirements, measure the opening and propose a complete door delivery including installation and commissioning.
