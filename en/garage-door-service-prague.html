When to call garage door service

  • The garage door will not open or close.
  • The operator does not respond to the remote control.
  • The door stops, rubs or makes an unusual sound.
  • You need adjustment, inspection, a new remote control or operator upgrade.

Fast diagnostics and a sensible solution

First we identify the cause and recommend a technically and financially reasonable solution. If adjustment or repair is enough, we repair. If the equipment is at the end of its life, we say clearly whether an upgrade or new custom-made garage door makes more sense.
