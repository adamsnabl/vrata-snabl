Contact inquiries

When you submit the inquiry form, the entered data are sent to adam@vratasnabl.cz through Web3Forms. A static website on GitHub Pages cannot send e-mails by itself, so the form uses an external form endpoint and shows a confirmation page after successful sending.

We do not send the customer name, phone number, e-mail address or message text to analytics.

Google Analytics and cookies

Google Analytics is loaded only after the visitor gives consent. It helps us understand visits and clicks on important contact actions such as phone, e-mail, map and inquiry buttons.

You can change the choice at any time using the cookie settings button.

Your rights

You may request information about the personal data related to your inquiry, correction or deletion where legally possible. For requests, write to adam@vratasnabl.cz.

Contact details are used only to handle the inquiry and related communication.
